陈述语气Indicative Mood

  • The rabbit ate the carrot.

祈使语气Imperative Mood

  • Rabbit, eat the carrot!
  • Please, give me thumbs-up!
  • 构成形式
    1用第二人称+一般现在时
    2取消第二人称+动词原形
    You eat the carrot -> Eat the carrot
    You don`t eat it -> Don`t eat it
    You are quiet -> -> Be quiet
    You never give up -> Never give up
    You give me coins -> Give me coins

虚拟语气

  • 与现在相反 条：过去式 主：would + v原 If I saw the rabbit now, I would bit him.
  • 与现在相反 条：过去式 主：would + v原 If I were you, I would give this video a thumbs-up.
  • 与将来相反 条：should + v原 主：would + v原 If I should see the rabbit tomorrow, I would bit him.
  • 与将来相反 条：should + v原 主：would + v原 If I should meet thee after long years, how should I greet thee?.
  • 与过去相反 条：had + 动过分 主：would have + 动过分 If I had seen the rabbit a few days ago, I would have bitten him.
  • 与过去相反 条：had + 动过分 主：would have + 动过分 If I had seen this video before, I would have aced my test.
  • 对现在的愿望：wish + 动过分 I wish (that) I were a rabbit.
  • 对过去的愿望：wish + had/would have + 动过分 I wish (that) I had eaten the carrot.
  • 对过去的愿望：wish + would/should/maight/could + v原 I wish (that) I could eat a rabbit.
  • 对现在的愿望：would rather + v原 I would rather that you didn`t eat a rabbit.

疑问语气

  • Did the rabbit eat the carrot?
  • Was the rabbit hungry?
